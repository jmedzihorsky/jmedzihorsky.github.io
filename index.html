About

     

I am a Fellow at the Department of Methodology, LSE and a Research Associate at the Varieties of Democracy Institute. I got my PhD at the Department of Political Science, Central European University.

I am a methodologist with a substantive focus on elections. My dissertation applied minimum mixture estimation to electoral fraud, seat allocation, group cohesion in legislatures, and text analysis. I also contribute to the methodologies of the Varieties of Democracy and Varieties of Party Indentity and Organization datasets.
