software

pistar
is an R package for the Rudas-Clogg-Lindsay $\pi^*$ mixture index of fit.

SetMethods
is an R package for a diverse group of techniques related to QCA, co-authored with Ioana-Elena Oana, Mario Quaranta, and Carsten Q. Schneider. It also includes replication data for Schneider & Wagemann (2012) Set-Theoretic Methods for the Social Sciences.
