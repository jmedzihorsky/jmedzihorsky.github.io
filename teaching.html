Teaching

I teach research design for master's students in data science. I have taught undergraduate and graduate courses in research design, quantitative methods, and African politics at Central European University and McDaniel College Budapest. I have also taught short courses and workshops in Bayesian statistics and computer assisted text analysis in several places, including the ECPR Summer School in Methods and Techniques and European University Institute. At LSE, I taught graduate seminars in Causal Inference for Observational and Experimental Studies and Applied Regression Analysis, and a doctoral workshop in matching. For more details, see my CV.
